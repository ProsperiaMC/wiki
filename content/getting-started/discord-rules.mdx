# Discord Rules

These rules apply to **all members** of the Prosperia Discord server. Failure to follow them may result in warnings, mutes, timeouts, or bans.

## General Conduct
1. Be respectful to all members and staff. Encourage positivity.
2. Ensure your Discord username, nickname, and profile picture are appropriate.
3. Do not spam messages, emojis, images, or reactions.
4. No self-promotion, solicitation, or advertisements.
5. Do not share personal or sensitive information (yours or othersâ€™).
6. Hate speech, harassment, or harmful language is strictly prohibited.
7. Political or religious discussions are not allowed.
8. Sexual, NSFW, or otherwise inappropriate content is prohibited.
9. Use all server bots respectfully and as intended.
10. Use channels only for their designated purposes.
11. Do not directly ping staff members. Allow them time to respond.

## Enforcement & Cross-Platform Bans
Failure to comply with these rules may result in disciplinary action at staff discretion.

**Important:**
If you are banned from either the Minecraft server or the Discord server, the ban may be applied to **both platforms**. Cross-platform bans are enforced to maintain community safety and integrity, with limited exceptions.

Appeals must be submitted through the Discord server.
