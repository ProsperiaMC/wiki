# Server Features

Prosperia expands on vanilla survival with carefully curated enhancements designed to improve depth, balance, and long-term engagement â€” without losing the core survival feel.

## World & Progression
- Custom world generation (biomes, structures, caves)
- Overhauled Nether and End dimensions
- Extended progression beyond vanilla limits

## Combat & Gameplay
- Legacy PVP combat (1.8-style combat)
- Custom enchantments
- MCMMO progression
- Graves system to reduce item loss frustration

## Economy & Nations
- Balance-focused economy
- Player ranks
- Jobs system
- Nations and wars (via Lands)
- SellWands
- Upgradable farms, hoppers, and spawners

## Quality of Life
- Backpacks
- Personal player tags
- Optimized systems to reduce grind
- No pay-to-win advantages

## Community & Support
- Reliable and active staff team
- Friendly and welcoming player community
- Fair enforcement of rules and systems

Each of these systems is documented in more detail throughout the rest of the documentation. Players are encouraged to familiarize themselves with the mechanics they interact with to avoid unintended rule violations.
